# Monetization Design

Monetization starts only after the buyer profile, seller search, property, invite, notification, and admin verification loop is working.

## v1 Candidates

- Buyer visibility upgrades for additional geography exposure.
- Seller invite credits with clear rate limits.
- Seller subscriptions for saved searches and higher monthly invite volume.
- Lender referral monetization after legal and partner workflows are confirmed.

## Exclusions

- No escrow.
- No earnest money custody.
- No automated offer acceptance.
- No transaction execution.
- No lender approval claims unless manually/admin verified.

## Acceptance Checks

- Pricing cannot imply Liber holds funds.
- Invite credits cannot bypass abuse controls.
- Visibility upgrades cannot create Fair Housing-risk targeting.
- Lender referrals must not require storing third-party credentials.
